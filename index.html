About Me

Hi, I'm Monica. I graduated from Iowa State University in 2018 with a Bachelor's of Science in Technical Communication.

In the age of tech, you see a lot of complex information out there. As a technical communicator, it's my job to do the behind-the-scenes work of deconstructing verbose scientific and engineering jargon and turn it into clear, concise content for the masses.

It's a skill that many companies unfortunately look over despite its ability to drastically improve customer satisfaction. When a product has truly helpful resources (documentation, training videos, brochures), customers are more likely to repeat business because they know that the company has invested in the infrastructure to help them.

This opportunity to not only help people, but also the companies themselves (big or small), is what drove me to love Technical Communication.
